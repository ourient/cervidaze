      <h2>accessibility</h2>
      <p>my goal is to make cervidaze as comfortable as possible to browse, no matter what device it's pulled from.
        i am someone particularly sensitive to suddenly loud and flashing stimuli, and i try not to
        replicate the same hostile experiences in my own space.</p>
      <p>my site will always be a work in progress, but this is what i have implemented so far:</p>
      <ul class="no-bullets">
        <li>mobile responsiveness</li>
        <li>properly contrasted site colors</li>
        <li>semantic html tagging</li>
        <li>dark mode toggle</li>
        <li>alt text for relevant images</li>
        <li>no auto-playing videos or audio</li>
        <li>static versions of my site button</li>
      </ul>
