terms of service

  • please don't hesitate to ask me for quotes or my capacity to draw certain subjects. i reserve the right to decline any request.
  • prices are listed in USD. your quote may change depending on the complexity of the commission. payments are received upfront and conducted through paypal. please leave the notes section empty and mark the transaction as for family/friends if prompted.
  • please expect to wait about 2 to 4 weeks to receive your commission. prints may take up to 1 week for domestic shipping and possibly longer for international shipping.
  • if you haven't received your prints and it's been past the estimated shipping date, please email me for assistance.
  • please credit me if you display my art online or in public. do not sell my art or distribute it under your name.

prints process

  • read through the terms of service. i'm not responsible for unexpected fees and agreements.
  • email me at c3rvidaze@pm.me with the subject line "art prints - YOUR NAME" with a brief description of the prints you want to order.
  • i will respond to you in about 2 - 3 days to coordinate shipping information.
  • once we have reached an agreement, payment will then be conducted upfront through paypal before i mail the prints.
  • the order will be shipped in a standard bubble mailer through the USPS. i will send you the tracking number if provided.

commissions process

  • read through the terms of service. i'm not responsible for unexpected fees and agreements.
  • email me at c3rvidaze@pm.me with the subject line "commission - YOUR NAME" with a brief description of what you'd like to commission, including the style (sketch, lineart, color, etc.) and subject(s). attaching references is strongly encouraged.
  • i will respond to you in about 2 - 3 days to coordinate. here, we will negotiate price and the scope of the commission.
  • once we have reached an agreement, payment will then be conducted upfront through paypal before i begin.
  • i'll send you a sketch for approval before proceeding with lineart and coloring. please let me know if you desire more WIPs during this process.
  • once completed, i'll email you the final file(s). please let me know if you prefer to have your commission exported in a specific format.
