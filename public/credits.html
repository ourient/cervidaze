about the site

cervidaze is hand-coded by me—with immense, immense amounts of help from designers much more experienced and tenacious than me. the site name is a reference to the family taxonomy for deer, cervidae.

please feel free to inspect my site to see how i scrapped everything together if something interests you-my email is also always open.


tools/assets

i use visual studio code and 11ty to code my site and then deploy it to neocities.

the header font is mondwest, and the body font is pixel operator.

i use coolors to select my site's color palettes. the site buttons are designed by me.

whenever possible, i include sources in the file names of my graphics, which are typically from unsplash and adobe stock (i am stealing with my workplace's license); otherwise, they are from tumblr, pinterest, or neocities sites. images are dithered and compressed with dithermark and tinypng respectively.

accessibility

my goal is to make cervidaze as comfortable as possible to browse, no matter what device it's pulled from. i am someone particularly sensitive to suddenly loud and flashing stimuli, and i try not to replicate the same hostile experiences in my own space.

my site will always be a work in progress, but this is what i have implemented so far:

  • mobile responsiveness
  • properly contrasted site colors
  • semantic html tagging
  • dark mode toggle
  • alt text for relevant images
  • no auto-playing videos or audio
  • static versions of my site button
